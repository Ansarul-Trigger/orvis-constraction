Structured Work Orders & Same-Day Reporting

We operate a formal work order and reporting system designed for transparency and accountability. Clients receive:

  • Same-day reporting
  • Photo documentation
  • Written work summaries
  • Completion and deficiency status
  • Organized records for audits and future reference

For multi-phase or complex projects, we utilize Gantt-based scheduling and milestone tracking to coordinate trades, manage timelines, and maintain schedule control.
