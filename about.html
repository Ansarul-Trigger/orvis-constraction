About Orvis Construction Inc.

A Calgary-based construction company focused on accountability, documentation, and long-term solutions.

Orvis Construction Inc. is a Calgary-based general contracting company specializing in residential, commercial, and property-management construction services. We deliver reliable renovations, additions, repairs, and ongoing maintenance with a strong focus on safety, documentation, and accountability.

Our team is Avetta compliant and actively completing ISNetworld certification, with a robust health, safety, and environmental program in place. We are known for identifying and resolving long-standing building issues, including leaks, structural concerns, and deferred maintenance that others miss.
